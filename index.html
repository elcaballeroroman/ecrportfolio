Born in Torreon, Mexico, Roman Caballero began taking selfies when he bought his first flip phone back in 2007 after graduating from high school and has no plans of ever stopping. Briefly interrupted by life struggles, photography has motivated him to seek to travel across North America, Europe, Asia and beyond. Although fashion is his specialty, he has also been accused of committing a variety of non-fashion related art. He is based in Coahuila and currently serving La Comarca area.
